5 Star Ballroom is North Nazimabad's premier event venue — home to two magnificent air-conditioned halls, Pearl and Diamond, each crafted to deliver flawless celebrations for up to 500 guests in style and splendour.

From lavish wedding receptions to vibrant mehndi nights and polished corporate events, our fully equipped halls, dedicated staff, and in-house catering team ensure every moment is handled with precision and grace.
